# FoodHub Data Analytics & Customer Insights Case Study

Data-driven analysis of online food delivery operations to optimise customer experience, improve operational efficiency, and support platform growth strategies.

## Project Overview
This project analyses FoodHub’s online food delivery dataset to uncover actionable insights across customer behaviour, order patterns, and operational performance.

The focus goes beyond descriptive analytics — translating data into strategic recommendations that can improve platform efficiency, customer retention, and revenue generation.

This case study demonstrates how analytics can be structured into clear, decision-ready insights for business and platform stakeholders.

## Business Problem
Online food delivery platforms operate in a highly competitive environment, where success depends on balancing customer satisfaction, delivery efficiency, and revenue growth.

Key challenges include:
- High delivery times → reduced customer satisfaction  
- Inefficient restaurant and order allocation  
- Lack of targeted customer engagement strategies  
- Missed opportunities for revenue optimisation  

The objective is to:
**leverage data to improve operational performance, enhance customer experience, and support scalable platform growth**

## Objective
Develop analytical insights to:
- understand customer ordering behaviour  
- identify key drivers affecting delivery performance  
- evaluate operational bottlenecks  
- improve targeting of promotions and engagement strategies  
- support data-driven decision-making for platform optimisation  

## Dataset
- Online food delivery transactions  
- Customer, restaurant, and order-level attributes  

Key variables include:
- order cost  
- delivery time  
- cuisine type  
- customer ratings  
- day and time of order  

## Approach
The project follows a structured analytics workflow:

1. Business understanding of platform operations  
2. Exploratory data analysis (EDA)  
3. Pattern identification in customer and order behaviour  
4. Analysis of operational metrics (delivery time, cost, ratings)  
5. Insight generation and business recommendations  

The focus is on interpretability and business relevance rather than purely technical modelling.

## Key Results
- Identified peak ordering periods and demand patterns  
- Highlighted relationship between delivery time and customer ratings  
- Uncovered key drivers influencing order value and frequency  
- Detected operational inefficiencies affecting service quality  
- Provided insights for targeted promotions and improved allocation strategies
